BEST MOVIES TO WATCH ACORDING TO ADITYA

My top 3 movies that i myself recommend

GONE GIRL

The narrative of "Gone Girl" is gripping and full of unexpected twists. It starts as a seemingly straightforward mystery about a woman's disappearance but evolves into a complex exploration of marriage, deception, and media influence. The plot keeps viewers on the edge of their seats, continuously re-evaluating characters and motives.

SHUTTER ISLAND

The narrative of "Shutter Island" is rich with psychological complexity and suspense. The story follows U.S. Marshal Teddy Daniels (Leonardo DiCaprio) as he investigates the disappearance of a patient from a mental institution on Shutter Island. The plot is filled with twists and turns, exploring themes of trauma, memory, and the blurred lines between reality and delusion. The psychological depth of the film keeps viewers engaged and continually guessing.

THE FAULT IN OUR STARS

The story of "The Fault in Our Stars" is deeply emotional and relatable, centering on the lives of Hazel Grace Lancaster (Shailene Woodley) and Augustus Waters (Ansel Elgort), two teenagers who meet and fall in love at a cancer support group. The film beautifully captures the highs and lows of their relationship, exploring themes of love, loss, and the meaning of life. It resonates with audiences by portraying the characters' experiences and emotions authentically.
